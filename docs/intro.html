We bring cryptographic trust to AI by validating computation proofs, inference attestations, and execution integrity signals.

Whether AI is running locally, in the cloud, within smart agent swarms, or across decentralized compute networks — ProofCortex allows users to verify what really happened, not just trust claims.

Why BlockDAG for ProofCortex?

Traditional blockchains are sequential, meaning they introduce latency, bottlenecks, and limited scalability for real-time AI verification.

BlockDAG (Directed Acyclic Graph-based architecture) solves these limitations.

How BlockDAG Fits ProofCortex

Challenge How BlockDAG Solves It Impact on ProofCortex
Sequential validation Parallel block propagation High-throughput proof verification
Limited concurrency DAG accepts asynchronous submissions Real-time multi-node model proof traffic
Network latency No single-chain head bottleneck Low-latency inference confirmation
Scalability Horizontal scaling with node growth Massive parallel AI pipelines
Off-chain trust gap DAG ordering preserves computation integrity Verifiable inference-to-proof lifecycle

In essence: BlockDAG = Proof Infrastructure for the AI Age.
